Understanding Your Water Source

The first step in choosing the right water purifier is understanding the quality of your water source. Water can come from various sources such as municipal supplies, borewells, rivers, or lakes. Each source has different levels of contaminants. Municipal water typically contains chlorine and other chemicals, while borewell water might have high levels of dissolved solids and minerals. Conduct a water test to determine the specific impurities present in your water supply.
